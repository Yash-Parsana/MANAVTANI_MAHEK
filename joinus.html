We are the collective of people who believe, together we can change the situation of street bound animals. Every day they suffer hunger, thirst, and negligence from humans. Every day some kind of animal abuse happens somewhere. Together we can truly change this situation of strays. It doesn't matter if you have 10 minutes, 1 hour or more, You can join us in several ways - feed strays, install water pots, rescue animals, raise donations or food, become an Animal Peers team member. Whatever you want to do, take action today and be part of the movement to care for stray animals.
